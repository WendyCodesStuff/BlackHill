Will my site be secure?


Although your site will be 'static' and therefore not vulnerable to data breaches (as it doesn't employ a database), the majority of website security breaches are not to steal or maniuplate data, but instead are attempts to use your server as an email relay for spam, or to set up a temporary web server. Hacking is regularly performed by automated scripts written to scour the internet in an attempt to exploit known website security issues in software. At Black Hill Web Design, we are constantly researching the ploys hackers use and we employ ever-evolving methods to mitigate these and stay one step ahead.

No web developer can promise 100% bulletproof security, but at Black Hill Web Design we can certainly dress your site in kevlar and give all known bots the slip.


Can you make a portfolio site?


If by 'portfolio' you mean an updateable catalogue of your latest works, then no. However, if you like the idea of a collection of non-updateable (fixed) works that will remain for the life of your website, then yes we can and do offer this.

Many of our sites, including our own front page include a gallery. A gallery showcases a catalogue of works but it is a static (fixed) gallery, more like an album than a dynamic portfolio.


How long will it take to build my website?


Depending on the size of your site, it can take between one and eight weeks to develop. Also, depending on our diary, there may occasionally be a backlog of a few weeks. The best thing to do is get in touch so we can give you a better idea.
