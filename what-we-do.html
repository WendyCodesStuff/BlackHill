What is a static website?


A static website contains web pages of fixed content. Each user sees the same content. As these websites do not include databases or dynamic content, they will perform quickly and are very lightweight. Other advantages include solid security, low maintenance, better availibility and fast recovery.

Static pages are making a real comeback and are ideal for customers who wish to show content that doesn't change. eg, a one-off campaign, business with set services, immutable content such as documentation or a newsletter.

According to dzone the six main reasons for chosing a static site are security, reliability, speed, hosting & price, scalability and recent technological advancements. They say "It is safe to say that static websites have made a terrific comeback and they are here to stay with more advantages than drawbacks. As a website is a digital face of the business, organizations must take into consideration all the factors and necessities of their business website and must assess all the new tools and platforms before making a website."
